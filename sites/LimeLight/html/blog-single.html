We Are Too Addicted To Our Gadgets!

june 3, 2013

Miko Ivan

Techonology

In the last study released, psychiatrists estimate that the total number of people who have become digitally dependent on their gadgets has risen by 300% over the past 5 years. If this trend continues, we are placing both our mental and physical well-being at high a risk. Most people have a difficult time letting go of their gadgets, with the continuous engagement social media and the continuous need for news updates.
